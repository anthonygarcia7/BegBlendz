About Beg

Anthony Garcia is the mastermind behind Beg Blendz. Using the alias given to him by his closest friends, Beg keeps his work professional and sharp.

Beg is a self-taught barber with great passion for the hair cutting practice and greatly values the connections made with his clientele during their visits to his shop. He has gotten to this point in his barber career thanks to the unwavering trust and support from his friends and family, whom have been available and willing to help Beg develop since the very beginning of his journey. Through their trust, Beg has been able to develop his skills, now being able to offer many modern and traditional hair styles for all individuals, including hair treatment processes such as hair perming and color dye.

He continues to further his techniques and skills, keeping up to date with the latest trends and styles, making sure all individuals that sit in his chair leave satisfied and feeling great about their new look.

Outside of his barbering career, Beg is currently a recent graduate of the University of Central Florida, with a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology. He aims pursue a career in the cybersecurity field.

With the introductions out of the way, come book an appointment, relax, and let Beg work his magic. As the slogan says, look good, feel better.
